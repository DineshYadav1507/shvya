Shvya Lead CRM — Full Feature Overview

A powerful CRM suite combining leads, invoicing, support, tasks, contracts, payments, surveys, and automation — built for modern businesses.

Central Dashboard

Smart KPI dashboard showing leads, sales, payments, projects, reminders & financial insights.

Leads & CRM

Lead pipeline, drag & drop stages, auto-import via email, web-to-lead forms and follow-ups.

Projects & Tasks

Projects, milestones, timers, Gantt chart, task followers, recurring tasks & productivity tracking.

Invoices & Estimates

Create invoices, estimates, recurring billing, multiple taxes, currencies, PDFs & reminders.

Payments & Expenses

Accept online payments, track expenses, convert expenses to invoices & manage payment records.

Inventory & Products

Manage items, units, item groups, taxes, and billable products synced with invoices & expenses.

Tickets & Knowledge Base

Email auto-import (IMAP), priorities, canned replies, attachments & article usefulness voting.

Reports & Analytics

Sales, expenses, leads conversion, customer reports, performance charts & custom date filters.

Clients & Contacts

Client portal with invoices, projects, tickets, files & multi-contact permissions per client.

Proposals & Contracts

Create proposals, track accept/decline, send PDFs, manage contract expiry & notifications.

Import & Export

Bulk import leads, customers, items & expenses via CSV. Export all major records instantly.

Roles & Permissions

Advanced role builder with module-wise permissions and overrides for staff access control.

Integrations & API

PayPal, Stripe, PayU, 2Checkout, SMTP, IMAP, SMS gateways, reCAPTCHA & REST API support.

Security & Encryption

Encrypted sensitive data, audit logs, IP logs, secure sessions & anti-bruteforce protection.

Templates & Styling

Custom email templates, PDF themes, portal styling and full color customization options.

Addons & Extensions

Chat module, appointments, dark/light themes, e-invoicing, SaaS module & many more plugins.

Goals & Tracking

Set revenue goals, conversion goals and measure staff performance in real-time.

Surveys & Feedback

Create surveys for staff, leads or customers to increase customer retention.

Events & Announcements

Staff events, public events, internal announcements and automated reminders.

Media Library

Separated folders per staff with secure file upload & sharing across projects.

Automation (Recurring)

Recurring invoices, tasks, expenses & reminders — fully automated scheduling.

Company Newsfeed

Internal activity feed for staff updates, communication and shared resources.

Staff Management

Manage departments, roles, staff reminders, productivity & internal communication.

Email Templates

Prebuilt email templates with merge fields & multi-language support.

Auto Backup & Tools

Automatic database backups, restore points & system requirement checks.

Hooks & Developer API

Extend Shvya Lead CRM using action hooks without modifying core files.
