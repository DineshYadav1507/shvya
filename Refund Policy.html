1. 7-Day Usage Period (After Deployment)

Once payment is made, Shvya deploys the purchased product/service. After deployment, clients receive a 7‑day usage period to evaluate systems such as:

  • Shvya CRM
  • Shvya AI
  • HRM + Payroll
  • Inventory System
  • School ERP
  • Hospital ERP
  • Restaurant ERP
  • Geo-Fencing System
  • Billing System
  • Web Development Demo
  • App Development Demo

2. 7-Day Refund Window (Post-Deployment)

Refunds can only be requested within 7 days after deployment.

  • No‑questions‑asked refund available
  • Service/operational fee may be deducted
  • Email refund requests to shvyaai@gmail.com

After 7 days, no refunds will be issued, even if:

  • System was unused
  • Client changed their mind
  • Business priorities changed

3. Non-Refundable Charges

  • Setup & Deployment Fee (INR 5000)
  • Custom development
  • UI/UX customization
  • Integrations/APIs
  • Add-ons
  • Custom-coded features
  • Third-party/hosting costs

4. Technical Issues

Refunds may be issued only if:

  • Issue is caused by Shvya
  • Severely impacts usage
  • Confirmed by Shvya technical team

Client-side or third‑party issues do not qualify.

5. Unauthorized or Fraudulent Transactions

If payment was unauthorized:

  • Contact shvyaai@gmail.com
  • Provide verification

A full refund will be issued once validated.

6. Cancellation Policy

  • No refunds after 7‑day period
  • No subscriptions/recurring payments
  • No partial/prorated refunds
  • Deployment fees become non‑refundable

7. How to Request a Refund

Email: shvyaai@gmail.com

Include:

  • Name
  • Email
  • Phone
  • Product purchased
  • Payment date
  • Transaction ID

8. Refund Processing Time

  • 7–10 business days
  • Refund via original payment method

9. Policy Updates

Shvya may update this policy anytime. Continued use signifies acceptance of changes.
