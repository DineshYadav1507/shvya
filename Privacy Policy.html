2. Scope & Overview

This Policy applies to personal data collected by Shvya through our website, forms, email communications, phone contact, public profiles and other services we provide. It also explains the lawful basis for processing, retention periods, security measures, and how to request deletion or other actions.

3. Personal Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal data, either directly from you or via third-party services you interact with:

Identity & Contact Data

  • First name, last name
  • Email address
  • Phone number

Profile & Interaction Data

  • Data you provide in contact forms, enquiries, support requests
  • Public profile information and any content you post or upload
  • Notes, messages and communication history with our team

Usage & Technical Data

  • IP address, browser type and version, device identifiers
  • Pages visited, time and date of visits, referring pages, clickstream data
  • Other system logs and diagnostic data

Other

Any additional data you voluntarily provide (e.g., attachments, documents)

Unless stated otherwise in the form or context of collection, providing requested personal data is voluntary but may be necessary for us to provide particular services (for example, responding to an enquiry).

4. How We Use Your Data (Purposes & Legal Bases)

We process personal data for the following purposes:

  • Contacting you / Responding to enquiries (Contact form, email, phone) — Legal basis: performance of a contract or legitimate interest to respond to enquiries.
    Data used: name, email, phone, message content.
  • Mailing list / Newsletters — Legal basis: consent.
    Data used: name, email. You may unsubscribe at any time.
  • Service delivery & support (proposals, product onboarding) — Legal basis: performance of contract and legitimate interest.
    Data used: contact, profile and interaction data relevant to the service.
  • Information collection & account management (user profiles, public profiles) — Legal basis: contract performance and legitimate interest.
    Data used: profile, usage data.
  • Security, fraud prevention and legal compliance — Legal basis: legitimate interests and compliance with legal obligations.
    Data used: technical & usage data, identity data.
  • Marketing & promotional communications — Legal basis: consent or legitimate interest (where allowed). You can opt-out at any time.
  • Operational & analytics purposes (improving site, product and services) — Legal basis: legitimate interest.
    Data used: usage and technical data.

5. Retention Periods

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purpose it was collected, or to comply with legal or regulatory obligations.

  • Data processed for contract performance: retained until the contractual relationship ends and any required retention period expires.
  • Marketing data (with consent): retained until consent is withdrawn.
  • Data processed for legitimate interests: retained as long as needed to fulfill those interests, subject to legal limits.

Once retention periods expire, data will be securely deleted or anonymized.

If you have specific questions about retention for particular data, contact us at shvyaai@gmail.com.

6. Data Sharing & Recipients

Your data may be shared with:

  • Our employees and authorized staff (administration, sales, marketing, system administration) who need it to perform their duties.
  • Third-party service providers acting as Data Processors (e.g., hosting providers, email platforms, analytics, CRM, payment processors). We require processors to take appropriate security measures and act only on our instructions.
  • Legal or governmental authorities where required by law or to protect our rights.

We maintain an up-to-date list of third-party processors which you may request by contacting us.

7. International Transfers

Some of our service providers and processors may be located outside your country. Where personal data is transferred internationally, we will ensure appropriate safeguards are in place (such as standard contractual clauses, or transfers to countries with adequate protection) to protect your data.

8. Security

We adopt reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration or destruction. Measures include access controls, encryption where appropriate, regular audits, and secure hosting environments. However, no system is 100% secure — if you suspect a security breach, contact us immediately.

9. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We and our third-party service providers use cookies and similar technologies to provide, analyse and improve our services. Cookies may be used to:

  • Store user preferences and session data
  • Enable website functionality

For full details, please see our Cookie Policy or the cookie notice presented when you first visit our site. You can manage cookie preferences via your browser settings.

10. Your Data Subject Rights

Where applicable (for example under the GDPR), you have the following rights in relation to your personal data:

  • Access: request confirmation whether we process your personal data and obtain a copy of that data.
  • Rectification: request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erasure (Right to be forgotten): request deletion of your personal data where there is no legal reason for us to retain it.
  • Restriction: request the restriction of processing in certain circumstances.
  • Portability: receive your data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format and transmit it to another controller where technically feasible.
  • Object: object to processing based on legitimate interests or for direct marketing.
  • Withdraw consent: where processing is based on consent, withdraw it at any time (this does not affect processing prior to withdrawal).
  • Lodge a complaint: with your competent data protection authority if you believe your rights are infringed.

To exercise any of the above rights, contact us at shvyaai@gmail.com. We will respond as required by applicable law and, in any event, within one month where legally mandated (extensions possible in complex cases).

11. Data Deletion Requests

If you wish to delete your data, email shvyaai@gmail.com with the subject line “Data Deletion Request” and include details (email, name, any identifiers). We will verify your identity and process the request in accordance with applicable law.

12. System Logs & Maintenance

We may collect system logs (e.g., IP addresses, server logs) for site security, debugging, and to maintain the service. Such data is used to detect abuse, troubleshoot and maintain availability.

13. Children

Our services are not directed to children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, contact us to request deletion.

14. Changes to This Privacy Policy
