1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites:

  • Recognize your device
  • Remember your preferences
  • Improve your browsing experience
  • Analyze website performance
  • Personalize content and ads

Cookies cannot access files on your device, harm your system, or collect personal information without your consent.

2. How We Use Cookies

Shvya uses cookies for several purposes. Below we summarise the main categories:

A. Essential & Functional Cookies

  • Enable core features such as page navigation, secure login (if applicable), form submissions, and site preferences.
  • Without these cookies, the website may not function properly.

B. Analytics & Performance Cookies

  • Help us understand how visitors use the website so we can improve it (pages visited, time on page, errors, traffic sources).
  • We may use tools like Google Analytics for this purpose.

C. Preference Cookies

  • Remember language settings, display preferences, and previously selected options for a smoother, personalised experience.

D. Marketing & Third-Party Cookies

  • Used only if applicable to show relevant ads, track ad performance, and measure campaign impact.
  • Examples include Meta Pixel, Google Ads, etc. You will only encounter marketing cookies if we run ads or tracking scripts on a specific page.

3. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies may come from third-party providers integrated into our website, such as Google (Analytics, reCAPTCHA), Meta (Facebook/Instagram Pixel), WhatsApp widgets, payment gateways, and embedded content. These third parties control their cookies and have separate privacy policies — Shvya does not control or access third-party cookie data.

4. Personal Data & Cookies

Some cookies may collect personal data such as device information, IP address, browser type, session identifiers, and interaction data. Any personal data collected through cookies is processed according to the Shvya Privacy Policy.

5. How to Manage or Disable Cookies

You can control or delete cookies at any time through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and other site data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions

You can block all cookies, allow only essential cookies, clear existing cookies, or block third-party cookies. Note: If you disable cookies, some features of the Shvya website may no longer work properly.

6. Consent

When you visit the Shvya website for the first time, you may see a cookie banner asking for your consent regarding non-essential cookies. By continuing to use the site after seeing the notice, you consent to our use of cookies as described. You may withdraw your consent at any time by adjusting your browser settings.

7. Updates to This Cookie Policy

Shvya may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in applicable laws, website functionality, or technology. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last Updated” date.
