Refund Policy

This page explains when refunds are available and how they are processed.

Overview

AlmostZero (the "Company") aims for customer satisfaction. This Refund Policy outlines the circumstances under which refunds are given, and how you can request one.

Eligibility

Refunds may be issued in the following situations:

  • The purchased product or service was not delivered as described.
  • A duplicate or erroneous charge was made.
  • Cancellation within the allowed window stated during purchase.

Timeframes: For digital goods, requests must be made within 14 days of purchase unless otherwise specified. For physical goods, returns must be initiated within 30 days of delivery.

Exceptions

The following are generally non-refundable:

  • Services fully completed with customer approval.
  • Items explicitly marked non-refundable at purchase.
  • Fees charged by third parties or for late payments.

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact our support team with the following details:

  • Order or invoice number
  • Purchase date
  • Reason for the refund request and any supporting evidence (screenshots, receipts)

We will review requests and respond within 5 business days. If approved, refunds will be issued to the original payment method and may take several business days to appear on your statement.

Contact

Please contact our support at: support@almostzero.io for refund requests or questions.
