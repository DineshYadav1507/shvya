Refund Policy

Clear, fair and easy — this policy explains when refunds are possible and how to request one.

Overview

AlmostZero (the “Company”) wants you to be satisfied with your purchase. This Refund Policy explains when refunds are available, how to request one, and what to expect when you do.

Eligibility for Refunds

Refunds may be issued if one of the following applies:

  • You purchased a product or service and it was not delivered as described.
  • A charge was made in error or duplicated.
  • You cancelled within the allowed cancellation window stated during purchase.

Timeframes: For digital products, refund requests must be submitted within 14 days of purchase unless otherwise stated. For physical goods, the return window is 30 days from delivery.

Exceptions & Non-Refundable Items

The following are typically non-refundable:

  • Services completed and delivered in full with customer approval.
  • Products or content explicitly marked non-refundable at time of purchase.
  • Charges for late fees, interest, or third-party services unrelated to the Company.

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please:

  1. Contact our support team at support@almostzero.io (or use the Request Refund button in the panel).
  2. Provide your order number, purchase date, and a brief reason for the request.
  3. Attach any supporting screenshots or documents that help explain the issue.

Once we receive your request, we will review and respond within 5 business days. If approved, refunds are issued to the original payment method and may take 5–10 business days to appear on your statement.

Frequently Asked Questions

What if I can't find my order number?

If you can’t find your order number, provide the email used during purchase and approximate purchase date — our team will help locate it.

How long until I receive my refund?

After approval, refunds are processed within 5–10 business days depending on your bank or payment provider.
